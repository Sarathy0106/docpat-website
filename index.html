Phase 1: Chunking

Divided into secure chunks.

To prevent single-point failures, the unified health record is shattered into 27 cryptographic pieces.

Phase 2: Distributing

Distributed across the network.

Each chunk is dispersed globally across decentralized IPFS nodes for maximum security and redundancy.

Phase 3: Securing

Verified & Immutable.

The network continuously verifies chunk integrity on-chain, ensuring zero tampering.

How It Works

Simple steps.
Maximum security.

From upload to verified share — your data stays yours every step of the way.

Step 01

Select & Upload.

Select your medical record from the file manager. The document is instantly encrypted client-side.

Step 02

Store & Hash.

The file is stored in the decentralized database. A unique cryptographic hashkey is generated via smart contract.

Step 03

QR Generation.

The hash is converted into a secure, PIN-protected QR code for easy physical and digital sharing.

Step 04

Instant Access.

Authorized scanning of the QR code decrypts the hash and retrieves the pristine document instantly.
