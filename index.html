How It Works

Simple steps.
Maximum security.

From upload to verified share — your data stays yours every step of the way.

Step 01

Add your record.

Snap it with your camera, pick it from your gallery, or choose a PDF. It's locked tight on your phone the moment you add it — before it goes anywhere.

Step 02

Stored safely. Sealed forever.

Your locked record is kept safe across a worldwide network, and stamped with a permanent digital seal that proves it's genuine.

Step 03

Make a QR code.

Create a QR code with its own PIN — valid for a day, a week, a month, or just one viewing. You decide how long it lives.

Step 04

Your doctor sees it. You stay in control.

They scan, enter the PIN, and wait for your approval. They can look — but never copy, screenshot, or keep your records. Take back access anytime.
