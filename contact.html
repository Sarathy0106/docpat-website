A note on what we can — and can't — see

Because your records are locked with keys only you hold, support can help with accounts, devices, and sharing, but we can never view, unlock, or recover your documents. If you lose all your devices and your recovery PIN, your records are unrecoverable by design — so please store your recovery PIN safely.
